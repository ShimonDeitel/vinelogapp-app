Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-07

Offline-first, no data collection

Vinelog stores all of your data -- entries, notes, and settings -- locally on your device using the file system. The app does not include analytics, tracking, advertising SDKs, or any network calls that transmit your data anywhere. We do not collect, sell, or share any personal information.

In-app purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We never see or store your payment information.

Your data

Because everything is stored locally, deleting the app deletes your data. There is no account, no cloud sync, and no server involved in the current version of Vinelog.
